How Do I Choose a Database?

Choosing from among the hundreds of databases that UCI owns can be daunting. Here are a few quick tips:

  • For general databases, try Databases to Get You Started.
  • For subject specific databases, try the Subject and Course Guides.
  • Ask a Librarian.
  • Use databases listed below as good starting points.
    • Academic Search Complete: Covers many different fields, includes scholarly and news articles.
    • Web of Knowledge: Focuses on scholarly articles.
